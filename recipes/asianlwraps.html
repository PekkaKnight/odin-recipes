Asian Lettuce Wrap

Description

These lettuce wraps were believed to have originated in China. They were made popular in P.F. Changs and now there are multiple resources saying how to make them. They are easy to recreate at home using any style you want.

Ingredients

  • 16 Boston Bibb or Butter Lettuce Leaves
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 Tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • A quarter clup hoisin sauce
  • 2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons minced pickled ginger
  • 1 dash Asian chile pepper sauce, or to taste(Your choice)
  • 1(8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained, and finely chopped
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons Asian(dark) sesame oil
