Summer Grilled Shrimp Salad

Description

This meal originated from Asia and has been popular around multiple asian restaurants! This make-at-home meal features sweet and smoky grilled shrimp and a cilantro vinaigrette to construe a great meal in the summertime, especially for those looking to eat healthy. Serve with some crusty bread and a nice glass of wine!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 and a quarter(2.25) teaspoons smokehouse maple seasoning(such as McCormick Grill Mates)
  • 1 and a half(1.5) teaspoons lemon juice
  • 12 ounces peeled and deveined shrimp
  • A quarter cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and Ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens, or more to taste
  • Half cup thinly sliced english cucumber
  • A third cup freshly cooked corn
  • A half cup diced tomato
  • A quarter cup sliced red onion
  • 1 avocado, diced
